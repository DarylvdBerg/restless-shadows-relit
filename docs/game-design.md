## Core Concept

You're playing as Pete, a child with severe nightmares. You must help Pete survive his several nightmares in order to wake up again.
The game should deliver the feeling of your 5~8 year old self having bad dreams.
Together with child-like weapons you'll face the nightmares.

---

## Core Loop

_The moment-to-moment action the player repeats. Keep it to one cycle._

1. **Do** — Use child like toys to progress through the nightmare levels.
2. **Get** — Points, resources as a reward and knoweledge about your surroundings
3. **Use** — Points & resources to craft upgrades.

---

## Mechanics

### Primary Mechanics

- **Flashlight:** Pete will use his flashlight to stun the enemies in his nightmare; this will allow him to hit and kill the enemies.
- **Shoot:** Pete will use several child toy like weapons to defend himself against the nightmares.
- **Generative levels:** each run of the game should not be the same.

### Secondary Mechanics

- **Crafting:** By doing runs in the game pete will gather points and resources. This will allow him to upgrade and buy new toys in his safe space (his bedroom)

## Win / Lose Conditions

- **Win:** The game is completed once all nightmare levels have been defeated and Pete wakes up from his nightmares.
- **Lose / Fail state:** You lose / fail if your HP / Hearts are 0.

---

## Progression

Like a traditional rogue like the very few first runs will be quite difficult. The player does A) not have any knowledge about the game and B) will have a very basic set of equipment. By doing runs over and over again, gathering resources and points, the player will be able to upgrade it's gear aka. making himself more powerful making it easier to progress through the earlier parts. This will be repeated throughout the game so far.
